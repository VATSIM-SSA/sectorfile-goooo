===== GOOOO Dakar Oceanic Flight Information Region =====

  * The Dakar Oceanic FIR covers an oceanic segment of the South Atlantic. It is neighbored by [[GOOO|Dakar]] FIR in the East, Sal Oceanic to the North and Altantico to the South.

==== Airport Coverage ====

  * Dakar Oceanic does not cover any airport.
